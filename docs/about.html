About Lake Charles Tree Service

Who We Are

Your Trusted Tree Service Professionals

Lake Charles Tree Service has proudly served Lake Charles and Calcasieu Parish for over 8 years. From routine tree trimming to emergency storm response, our licensed and insured crews deliver professional tree care you can count on.

Living in Southwest Louisiana means dealing with hurricanes, tropical storms, and severe weather. Our storm-ready crews are trained, equipped, and available 24/7 to respond when disaster strikes. We understand the urgency of fallen trees, damaged limbs, and hazardous situations that follow major weather events.

With professional-grade equipment and a commitment to safety, we handle every job with precision and care. Whether it's a single tree removal, complete lot clearing, or post-storm cleanup across multiple properties, we have the expertise and resources to get the job done right.

We're proud to be part of the Lake Charles community. Our reputation has been built on honest pricing, reliable service, and treating every property like our own. With over 500 jobs completed and a 5-star rating, our work speaks for itself.
