Professional Tree Service for Lake Charles Residents

Lake Charles Tree Service is the area's most trusted provider of comprehensive tree care throughout Lake Charles and greater Calcasieu Parish. As the parish seat and the largest city in southwest Louisiana, Lake Charles presents unique challenges for tree maintenance. From the towering live oaks that line residential streets near the lakefront to the dense pine stands in newer subdivisions, our team has the equipment and expertise to handle every situation safely and efficiently.

Our city sits along the banks of the Calcasieu River and the shores of Lake Charles itself, creating a subtropical environment where trees grow vigorously year-round. That same climate also puts us squarely in the path of Gulf hurricanes and severe weather. After the devastating impacts of Hurricanes Laura and Delta, property owners across Lake Charles understand the critical importance of proactive tree maintenance. Regular trimming and hazard assessment can prevent catastrophic damage during storm season, saving homeowners thousands of dollars in emergency repairs and property damage.

Whether you own a home in the Country Club neighborhood, manage commercial property along Ryan Street, or maintain acreage off Nelson Road, Lake Charles Tree Service delivers professional results backed by full licensing and insurance. We take pride in keeping Lake Charles properties safe, beautiful, and storm-ready throughout every season.

Our Tree Services in Lake Charles

We provide a full range of professional tree services to Lake Charles residents and businesses:
